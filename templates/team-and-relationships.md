## Bob
- **Role:** COO, Hashworks Industries (partner of the multi-family office)
- **Relationship:** Andrew's direct superior and closest working relationship in the firm. Andrew is Bob's proxy — they think the same way.
- **What Bob does:** Handles finance, operations, legal, execution. Keeps the ship running.
- **Context:** Bob and Andrew co-built the core HashX lending system together. Tightest relationship in the firm. Bob trusts Andrew with everything — including on the crypto hedge fund thesis. Andrew has been building internal buy-in for the hedge fund primarily through Bob as an internal champion.

## Todd (Founding/Managing Partner)
- **Role:** Founding/managing partner of the multi-family office. CEO and visionary. Owns the majority of the business.
- **Relationship:** Senior decision-maker; key person Andrew needs to navigate for the hedge fund proposal. Andrew grew up next door to Todd — babysat his kids for 6-8 years. Todd randomly texted Andrew when Andrew was job hunting (looking for Chief of Staff roles in tech, on hiatus after a failed startup). Todd was impressed when Andrew told him about 10x'ing a trade longing Covid during the crypto bear market, and hired him to work with Bob as an analyst buying a bitcoin miner.
- **Background:** Ex-commodity trader. History building energy-adjacent businesses. Only understands that vernacular — if it's not energy, it doesn't compute. Used to being in the middle of picks-and-shovels plays. Will be directional on energy, but not crypto.
- **What he does for the firm:** The visionary and sales lead — can convince people to do things others can't.
- **Communication style:** Doesn't like to read or get in the weeds. Forms strong opinions fast — gut person who gets very passionate in either direction quickly. Once he's blocked on something, it takes an outside force (internal champion, podcast he trusts) to open his mind. If he's interested in a concept, he'll greenlight immediately.
- **Key dynamic:** Andrew has never been in a position to push Todd into something Todd is skeptical on from the jump. The path is more likely through other partners opting in and/or external sources shifting his frame.
- **Decision authority:** Does NOT have full sway — other partners can opt into things independently. But owns the majority of the business.
- **Personal relationship:** Incredibly giving person. Known Andrew since high school. The relationship predates the firm.
