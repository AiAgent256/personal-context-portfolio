## AI/Agentic Services Business Thesis
- **What:** Andrew has identified an opportunity for the firm to offer AI implementation and agentic services to middle-market enterprises. The thesis: the gap between what's possible with AI and how typical white-collar workers/enterprises use it is massive. Even as models get easier, the volume of information and pace of change means SMEs/experts will need to translate AI possibilities for those outside the know. Second-order thinking: most people seeing this opportunity are AI-native but lack ops/compliance/security expertise. Hashworks has a COO (ops/compliance) and CISO (cybersecurity) — implementation of agentic frameworks is only one piece; making them compliant and secure at the enterprise level is more important.
- **Status:** Early — thesis developed, business plan in progress. This is one of the three Haven256 sub-verticals (AI services), not a separate initiative.
- **Andrew's role:** Strategic architect
- **Priority:** Part of the broader Haven256 expansion — one of the three sub-vertical business plans being built this week
